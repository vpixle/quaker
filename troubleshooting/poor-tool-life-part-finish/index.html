Poor tool life / Part finish

Insufficient Cooling

  • Poor fluid flow
  • Improper fluid nozzle direction
  • Entrained air (foam)
  • Fluid temperature too high
  • Concentration too high
  • Excessive tramp oil

Tooling

  • New supplier
  • Unsharp or wrongly selected tools
  • Poor alignment
  • Wrong cutting parameters
  • Build-up on tooling
  • Wheel loading

Part

  • Change in alloy (Hardness, porosity)
  • Design change

Insufficient Lubrication

  • Low concentration
  • Depletion of lubricant
  • Entrained air (foam)
  • Excessive tramp oil

Lack of detergency

  • Excessive tramp oil
  • Depletion of detergent additives
  • Build-up on tooling
  • Wheel loading
